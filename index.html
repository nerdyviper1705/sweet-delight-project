Our Journey of Flavor and Comfort

In 1989, Emily and Liam Carter opened our doors with a dream that started back in college. They wanted to create a special place where everyone—students, professionals, families, and friends—could come together, enjoy great coffee, and savor delicious cakes.

Emily ’s passion for baking and Liam ’s love for coffee inspired them to turn their dream into reality. They found an old bookstore in San Francisco and transformed it into a cozy café that feels like home. They designed the space with something for everyone: comfy corners for quiet study sessions, spacious areas for meetings, and vibrant spots where kids can play and families can gather.

The walls of Sweet Delight Café are adorned with local artwork, and the air is always filled with the tempting smell of freshly brewed coffee and homemade treats. Emily and Liam’s goal was to create a welcoming atmosphere where every visit is a pleasant experience, and they’ve done just that.

At Sweet Delight Café, we’re more than just a place to eat and drink—we’re a place to make memories. We invite you to join us and enjoy the sweet moments that life has to offer.

Say Hello to Our Passionate Team!

Emily Carter-Bennett

Founder & Head Baker

Emily is the creative mastermind behind Sweet Delight. With a lifelong passion for baking, she crafts the cakes and pastries that have become the hallmark of the bakery. Emily’s vision is to make every celebration sweeter, and she personally ensures each product reflects her commitment to quality and flavor. Her attention to detail and love for the craft have made Sweet Delight a go-to destination for cake lovers.

Liam Bennett

Co-owner & Coffee Program Director

As the co-owner and head of coffee operations, Liam has turned Sweet Delight into a one-stop destination for both gourmet cakes and exceptional coffee. He curates a menu of specialty coffee blends and works closely with suppliers to bring the best beans to their customers. Liam is dedicated to delivering an unforgettable coffee experience that pairs perfectly with the bakery’s desserts.

Maya Singh

Client Relations & Event Manager

Maya is the face of Sweet Delight’s customer relations and event planning. With her warm demeanor and organizational expertise, she works closely with clients to create memorable events, from weddings to corporate gatherings, ensuring the perfect cake and coffee pairing for every occasion. Maya’s ability to connect with customers and her flair for event coordination make her indispensable to the team.
